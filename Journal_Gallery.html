Amsterdam

Netherlands

This canal lined city houses several reputed museums - the Van Gogh, Rembrandt House, the Anne Frank House, Rijksmuseum among others. You could navigate around on a bike like the locals, visit the famed Vondelpark, or ice skate at the city sqaure.

Tétouan

Morocco


In Tétouan, a northern port city, about 65 km from east of Tangier, a grasroots movement is redefining Moroccon art. Contempoarary artists such as Safaa Erruas, whose ethereal paper installations incorporate, pins, needles and other dressmaking elements, find community at such places as Green Olive Arts. Here visitors can meet artists at open studio events.


Côte d'Azur

South-East France


Also known as the French Riviera, this dreamy coastline of southern France is sprinkled with some of the world’s most popular beaches and destinations, including Monaco, Nice, Cannes and Saint Tropez. Enjoy a sunny afternnon at one of the numerous beaches, indulge in shoppping along the coastal markets, or experience glamour at the annual Cannes Film Festival!.

Sky Lantern Festival

Pingxi, Taiwan


The Discovery Channels ‘Fantastic Festivals of the World’ show has highlighted the Lantern Festival as one of the best festivals in the world and something everyone should try and experience once in their lifetime.
