Bienvenue!

Le Monde is a one of a kind magazine that gives you a peek into a myriad of remarkable cities, their food, culture and lifestyle.

Here at Le Monde, we are a group of passionate travellers who absolutely love exploring the riches that our world has to offer. From the sites of global repute to nooks and corners of lesser known towns, we like to experience it all: heritage, art, festivities, and especially food...discovering places that are unique and inspiring in their own ways.

Here we document our experiences, with pictures and personal annotations for you and other voyagers like us, to plan their next travel or simply for the joy of reading.

Regards, Team L.M.

Featured Stories :


Santorini

Greece

Santorini is the supermodel of the Greek islands, a head-turner whose face is instantly recognisable around the world: multicoloured cliffs soar out of a sea-drowned caldera, topped by drifts of whitewashed buildings. With its reputation for dazzling panoramas, romantic sunsets and volcanic-sand beaches.

Yosemite National Park

California, USA

Yosemite lies in the Sierra Nevada mountains. It is famed for its giant, ancient sequoia trees, and for Tunnel View, the iconic vista of towering Bridalveil Fall and the granite cliffs of El Capitan and Half Dome. In Yosemite Village are shops, restaurants, lodging, the Yosemite Museum and the Ansel Adams Gallery.

The Temple Bar

Dublin, Ireland

Temple Bar is a busy riverside neighbourhood, spread over cobbled pedestrian lanes. Crowded pubs host live folk music and DJ sets, and diners pack restaurants serving Asian, American and Irish cuisine. Quirky boutiques stock clothes and crafts by local designers.
